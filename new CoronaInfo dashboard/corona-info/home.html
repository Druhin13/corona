What's the source ?

The data is gathered from the State bulletins and Gov. official handles. They are further validated by a group of volunteers from covid19india.org
MoHFW
Ministry of Health and Family Welfare
* MoHFW updates their data at a scheduled time. But CoronaInfo updates its data regarding the state press bulletins, Official Gov. handles' posts , PBI news, Press Trust of India & ANI reports, etc. CoronaInfo refreshes its data every minute
ICMR
Indian Council of Medical Research
* Patients age data is gathered from ICMR
